About Nehkagrata

Nehkagrata is a brand founded by Neha Bhatia. Neha is an Anchor in the event Industry from past 7 years. She started her Anchoring Career after taking the Graduation Degree in Mass Communication and journalism. And in the year 2015, she went on a mission to train people on Pubic Speaking and Leadership and transform them for their better future. She has trained the youngsters in the Colleges and she has taken numerous Independent Workshops where people from different backgrounds participated. She had not just created public speakers or leaders but played a very important role in directing them for their vision and goals. Also being a certified Neuro Linguistic Programming Coach, she has helped many people in addressing their psychological, emotional, professional personal and physical challenges. This also includes challenges like clarity in career, relationship challenges, anxiety, habits, behavioural challenges. She further contributed in making them a leader and a visionary through some very intense and interesting Neuro Linguistic Programming techniques that are very scientific, intense and highly practical. She also feels if she creates at least 10000 extremely influential leaders in next 10 years, she will be able to contribute in people’s lives and country’s growth. She is also a yoga instructor trained from many institutes and organizations. Nehkagrata comes from Neha and Ekagrata. Ekagrata means focus or one-pointedness. “A man without eyes is blind but a man without a vision is dead”. Her vision is to make everyone a leader and a visionary. She strongly believes that it is possible only by removing psychological, emotional and physical challenges of the people. Through these sessions and coaching, she has been contributing in many people lives and making the society and country a better place to live in, yet she continues to host, anchor and entertain her audience through her intelligence, humour and presence.
